Road Trip is Fun

I try to make time for a raod trip every year, usually during the summer times. My goal is drive across all 50 states. So far, my favorite roads are U.S. 101 (northern California to Central Oregon), U.S. 1 (Miami to Key West), Beartooth Highway (MT/WY), Old McKenzie Highway (OR). I have been to 21 national parks and my favorites are Yellow Stone, Glacier, and Crater Lake.
