Bike Grand Teton National Park

Grand Teton national park has a incredible biking trail that starts from Jenny Lake and goes all the way into Jackson, WY. The trail is paved and completely separated from the park road. This is a perfect way to explore the park. Heck, you could also bring your backpacking gear and make it a bikepakcing trip. However, if you plan to visit during the summer peak season like I did, be prepare for the massive crowd on the trail.
