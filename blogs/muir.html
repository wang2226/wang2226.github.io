Hike to Camp Muir

Mount Rainier has always loomed large, both on the Washington skyline and in the minds of those drawn to alpine adventure. Towering at 14,410 feet, this glaciated giant challenges and inspires in equal measure. Among its most iconic hikes is the route to Camp Muir, a rugged base camp perched at 10,188 feet. This journey isn't just a hike, it’s an ascent into a world of snowfields, shifting clouds, and raw mountain beauty.
